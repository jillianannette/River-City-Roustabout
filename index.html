River City Roustabout

working man music to get you through the week

band

River City Roustabout is a Cincinnati-based folk band,

 drawing inspiration from the roustabout workers along the muddy banks of the Ohio River.

Luke Jellison

vocals, guitar, harmonica, kazoo, spoons


Jeremy Bartel

bass, mandolin, guitar


Scott Smith

vocals, percussion, mandolin, bass


Bryan Heeb

banjo, percussion


Adam LaVigne

percussion, backup vocals


Stephen Rees

mandolin


music

With an authentic folk sound,

 their songs explore self-doubt, dreams of letting go, and hitting the road.
